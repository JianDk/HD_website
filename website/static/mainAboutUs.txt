Hidden Dimsum Nytorv is our main dimsum house. This is the dimsum house where it all started, and where Michelin chefs around Copenhagen often pass by to experience our handmade dimsum and the vibe by themselves. Table booking is often necessary as under normal circumstances this dimsum house will be running with full house every day. 

Hidden Dimsum by Night was established in 2019 and is an Asian latenight dimsum and beer bar. If you love Hongkong style dimsum street food with K-pop and a broad selection of Asian beers and cocktail this is the place for you. 

Hidden Dimsum 2900 was established in 2020 and mainly intended for dimsum takeaway. It has a different dimsum selection as compared with Hidden Dimsum Nytorv and the wall decoration reflects our history and root. 

Dimsum Box is a dinner box containing handmade frozen dimsum that you can order online and delivered right to your front door.
